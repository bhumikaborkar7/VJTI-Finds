⚙️ How It Works


  1. Report an Item:
    If you've misplaced something or discovered an item on campus, you can easily report it through our platform. Simply choose whether the item is lost or found and start the process in just a few clicks. This helps create a centralized system where all items are tracked efficiently.
  2. Add Item Details:
    Provide clear and accurate information about the item to increase the chances of a successful match. Include details such as the item's name, description, category, location where it was lost or found, and the date. You can also upload an image to make identification easier and faster for other users.
  3. Search & Browse:
    Users can explore the platform by browsing through all reported items or using the search feature to find something specific. Filters such as category, date, or location make it easier to narrow down results and quickly locate relevant items.
  4. Connect & Claim:
    When a potential match is found, users can securely contact the person who reported the item. After verifying the details, the rightful owner can arrange to claim their item. An email will be sent to the person whom you want to contact, and they can respond to you directly. This ensures a smooth, safe, and reliable handover process.

📖 About


VJTI Finds is a simple and efficient platform created to help students and staff at VJTI reconnect with their lost belongings.
Losing personal items on campus can be frustrating, and finding them again often depends on chance — this platform makes the process easier and more organized.


Users can quickly report lost or found items, browse through listings, and connect with others to recover their belongings.
By bringing everything into one place, VJTI Finds reduces confusion and increases the chances of successful returns.


Our aim is to build a helpful and responsible campus community where students support each other and ensure that lost items find their way back to their rightful owners.
